FAQ

Mining

Only way to get BYZ coins outside of an exchange, or the limited BLUE swap is by mining them. BYZ is SHA256 mineable, with a variable reward per block dependent on the whole network hashpower and luck. BYZ miners mine Bitcoin via the Byzantine proxy, and the amount their mine is distributed proportionally amongst the BYZ stakers. In exchange they receive newly minted BYZ coins.

SuperStaking

Superstaking enables the BYZ network to have a gigantic throughput of information needed to make a Bitcoin side channel as well as a vehicle for 2FA and BYZ proxy mining. Superstakers delegate their coins and receive credentials to their hosted supernode which passes more information than a normal node, because its hosted on the proper infrastructure to do so. This process is irreversible. Superstaking or 10x staking is a optional method in which the user can stake any amount of their coins, and receive proportionally 10x the amount of coins.

D2FA

D2FA is Decentralized Two Factor authentification is a highly secure TOTP two factor authentification protocol running on the BYZ network. No more servers, no more central failure points. BYZ D2FA was a highly guarded technology from Blue team, we needed to create a new kind of super-fast blockchain with enough throughput to finally realize this vision. Our version of D2FA will be ready to plug and play into any software shortly after BYZ networks successful launch.

Referral System

Superstaking is highly important to the throughput BYZ network, thats why we implemented additional incentives to spread the knowledge about it and about BYZ in the form of a-in-blockchain referral system. People referring others will receive 5% of the superstaking amount, as well as the people their refer. The actual superstaking amount is 11x, 0.5x to referral and 0.5x to the person being referred. A person not referred will not receive this bonuses. This feature will be "baked in" to the blockchain and there for ever.

Exchanges

BYZ is a tradable coin, which gets its addressing structure from Bitcoin and other coins, therefor its very easy to add it to exchanges. BYZ will ship with all the documents needed to add it to any exchange in 5minutes. No developer permission necessary.
